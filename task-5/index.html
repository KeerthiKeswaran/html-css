Minimalist design is a philosophy centered on simplicity and clarity. By stripping away non-essential elements, designers focus on the core message.

Your brain is an energy-demanding organ that requires the right nutrients to function at its peak. Foods rich in omega-3 and antioxidants are proven to boost focus.
